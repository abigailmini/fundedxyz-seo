Payout Process Explained

Here's exactly how payouts work at FundedXYZ:

  1. Request withdrawal via your FundedXYZ dashboard
  2. Review period — 1–2 business days for the compliance team to verify
  3. USDT sent to your specified wallet address
  4. Total processing time: 1–5 days (typically 1-2 days)

  6. No withdrawal fees from FundedXYZ (network gas fees apply)

Request payouts to a Tron (TRC-20) USDT address for the lowest gas fees. ERC-20 USDT works too but gas fees will be higher. Most traders use Binance or OKX for receiving USDT.
