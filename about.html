About Us

Empowering Traders Worldwide

FundedXYZ was built with a simple belief: talented traders shouldn't be held back by a lack of capital. We provide the funding — you provide the skill.

Meet the Founder

Matthew McGregor

CEO & Founder

Matthew McGregor is the visionary behind FundedXYZ. With years of experience in the cryptocurrency and financial markets, Matthew saw a gap in the prop trading space — talented crypto traders who lacked the capital to trade at scale.

Driven by a passion for democratizing access to capital, Matthew founded FundedXYZ to create a fair, transparent, and scalable platform where skilled traders can prove themselves and earn real profits without risking their own money.

Under Matthew's leadership, FundedXYZ has grown into one of the most trusted crypto prop trading firms, backed by deep VC and hedge fund capital. His vision is simple: identify great traders, fund them generously, and build long-term partnerships that benefit everyone.

Our Mission

Democratize Trading Capital

We believe great traders exist everywhere — not just in institutional settings. FundedXYZ provides up to $100K in trading capital to anyone who can prove their skill, regardless of background.

Backed by venture capital and hedge fund partners, FundedXYZ isn't a fly-by-night operation. We're built with institutional-grade infrastructure and deep liquidity to support thousands of funded traders simultaneously.

Trader-First Approach

Every decision we make starts with the question: "Is this good for our traders?" From our generous profit splits to our unlimited time policy, we align our success with yours.
