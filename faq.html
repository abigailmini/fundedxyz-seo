Trading Rules

The profit target is 8% of your account balance. For example, on a $25,000 account, you need to generate $2,000 in profit to pass the challenge.
The maximum overall loss is 10% of your starting account balance. If your account drops below this threshold, the challenge is failed. Additionally, there is a 5% maximum daily loss limit to prevent excessive risk on any single day.
The daily max profit is capped at 3% of your account balance per day. This encourages consistent trading rather than gambling on high-risk trades. Profits above 3% in a single day won't count toward your target.
All accounts come with up to 5x (1:5) leverage. This provides enough buying power to make meaningful trades while keeping risk manageable. The leverage applies across all supported crypto trading pairs.
FundedXYZ supports trading on major cryptocurrency pairs including BTC, ETH, SOL, and other popular altcoins. The full list of available pairs is shown on our trading platform once you start your challenge.

There's no limit on payout frequency. Once you have profits in your funded account and meet the minimum payout threshold, you can request a withdrawal at any time.
